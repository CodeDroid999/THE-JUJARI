Shipping & Delivery Policy
 

Shipping Countries:
Kenya

 

Handing Time: 1-3  Working Days.
Fulfilled: Mon-Sat.

 

Delivery time:
Main Cities: 1 - 2 Working Days;
Regional Areas: 2 - 5 Working Days;
All Other Areas: 3 - 7 Working Days.
Fulfilled: Mon-Sat.

 

Please take note of the following:
You'll receive your tracking number via email;
All orders are sent by courier;
Delivery services are door-to-door, no PO Boxes please.

For Return And Refund, you can also find more information on Return & Refund page.

 

Shipping Cost:

FREE shipping for all orders above KES1500. (Remote Areas not Included)

If the order price is less than KES1500, there will be an additional shipping fee: KES300.
