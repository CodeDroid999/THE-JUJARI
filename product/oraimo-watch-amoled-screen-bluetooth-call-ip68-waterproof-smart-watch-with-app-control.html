Product Features:

1.95'' AMOLED Display

Upgraded Visuals, Sleek Design

The TheJUJARIWatch ES 2 adopts a 1.95'' AMOLDED screen, which can provide you with extremely smooth operations, and more saturated and vivid colors. Togethered with the simplistic and large icon display, the watch ensures a comfortable upgraded visual experience.

 

24-hour Health Monitoring

Wellness Tracker—TheJUJARIHealth App

The TheJUJARIWatch ES 2 supports Heart rate, Blood oxygen, Respiratory rate, Stress, Sleep status self-monitoring as well as a period tracker, helping with health problem detection. Download the TheJUJARIHealth app for a better experience in managing your health.

 

Operational Convenience

Upgrade Your Communication

Quick Reply SMSes

At one tap, a customized message will be sent when you hang up an incoming call, letting the others know that you are busy.

Quick Callback

You can directly call back on the Call History page.

Up to 100 Contacts

With an optimized number of contacts, follow up with every person you meet on a business occasion.

 

100+ Sport Modes

Setting Your Fitness Goals

The TheJUJARIWatch ES 2 supports 100+ sport modes such as outdoor running, treadmill, and bicycling, meeting your diverse exercise needs. The watch accurately records your exercise status, and allows one-click sharing of exercise data and scenery encountered in sports to social media.

 

Up to 7-day Using Time

Fast Charge and Go

You can charge the watch during your morning routine when taking a shower or brushing your teeth. Its lithium polymer battery can hold up to 24 hours on a fast charge and up to 7 days on a full charge of 3 hours.

*Using Time: 7 days for typical use, 5 days for moderate use, and 3 days for heavy use.

 

Useful Gadgets

Your Sweet Personal Assistant

Discover more useful gadgets for daily life, such as setting an agenda reminder, checking world clock and weather, remote control of camera, and flashlight.

IP68 Waterproof & Dustproof
Explore the Outdoors Without Limits

Have no fear of water splash or sudden rain, as your watch is well-protected with an IP68 rating for splash, water, and dust resistance.
