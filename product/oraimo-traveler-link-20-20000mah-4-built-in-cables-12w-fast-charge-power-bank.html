Product Features:

4 Built-in Charging & Recharging Cables

Seamless Power Connectivity

 

Traveler Link 20 comes with built-in Type-C, Micro-USB, and Lightning charging cables to meet your daily charging needs. What's more, a USB-A cable is included for easy recharging.

Durable Cable

Built to Last

The thickened wire protection and anti-winding process make the built-in charging cable more durable and rugged. Moreover, a higher charging conversion rate can be achieved thanks to pure copper conductor utilization. 

4 Output Ports

Charge Up to 4 Devices Simultaneously

Traveler Link 20 is equipped with 3 built-in charging cables and 1 USB-A port, Traveler Link 20 can simultaneously deliver power to 4 devices at a speed of up to 12W.

3 Input Ports

Recharge Your Power Bank Flexibly

Traveler Link 20 can be recharged using a Type-C port, Micro-USB port, or a USB-A cable, providing multiple recharging options for convenience.

20000mAh Big Capacity

Fully Charge Your Phone Up to 5 Times

Featuring a high battery capacity, Traveler Link 20 can fully juice up an iPh XS almost 5 times.

Endurance LED Torch

Up to x Hours of Illumination

Enjoy up to x hours of illumination to handle any unexpected emergency with ease.

*Long press the main button for 3 seconds to open the LED torch.

Wide Compatibility

Smart and Powerful

Equipped with oraimo's exclusive chip, AniFast™ power bank can intelligently identify the fast-charging protocol of your device to deliver the speediest possible charge.
