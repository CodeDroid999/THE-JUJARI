Product Features:


Pro Hybrid Active Noise Cancellation
Focus on Your Own Zone

Pro Hybrid ANC technology combines traditional ANC technology with a proprietary algorithm to produce superior audio performance, can get rid of up to 45dB noise, allowing you to enjoy a more immersive audio experience with fewer distractions.

Transparency for Ambient Awareness
Listen to Your Surroundings

Necklace Pro provides you with the best of both worlds - you can enjoy the natural ambient sound when you want to be engaged with your environment, enabling you to have a conversation or be aware of the outside world.

Intelligent Magnetic Switch
Free Your Hands

Necklace Pro will automatically power off when the earbuds are snapped together and automatically power on when separated. This convenient feature allows you to get away from tangle-free carrying, get easy storage, and make an intelligent switch.

13mm Composite Large Dynamic Driver
Incredibly Immersive Listening Experience

The increased bass response and superior sound clarity that come with a large dynamic driver size make for an incredibly immersive audio experience. Every moment of listening is filled with clear highs, mids, and deep, powerful bass.

50-hr Battery Life
Ultra Long-lasting Battery

Improved with 350mAh lithium battery, it can last for 50 hours with one full charge. The long battery life makes it perfect for those long days at the office, studying, or even road trips.

50H playtime in ANC OFF mode
32H playtime in ANC ON mode

Charge 10-min, Play 10-hr
Say Goodbye to Low Battery Anxiety

Fast charging technology can rapidly recharge your Necklace Pro in a very brief amount of time. Get 10 hours of music playback with only a 10-minute charge. Save your charging time and experience the joy of music!

Crystal Clear Calls
Inline Noise-cancelling Microphone

AI algorithms and the specially-design inline microphone placed close to your mouth can ensure that your voice is faithfully transmitted to the person you are calling, guaranteeing you can easily understand the other person no matter where you are.

Dual Device Connection
Freely Switch to the Other Devices

Necklace Pro can connect 2 devices at the same time. Never worry about missing calls from another phone.

*Long press the button "O" and "+" at the same time for 2s to connect to the second device.

IPX5 Waterproof & Sweat Protection
Not Afraid of Water and Sweat

Featured with IPX5 waterproof and inner Sweat-Resistant Nano-Coating, Necklace Pro is created to withstand the test of time, accompanying you through rain and workout time.

Low-latency Game Mode
Audio and Video Sync in Game

Game mode provides fluent experience while playing video or games, helping you capture your best gaming moments.

Dual EQ
Switch As You Like

Long press the Mode button "M" to switch between Normal Mode and HavyBass™ Mode.

Secure Fit Design
Comfortable and Durable Wearing Experience

Ergonomically designed and crafted with ultra-soft, high-grade materials, Necklace Pro will provide you with a comfortable wearing experience. No fear to different twists and distortions with in-built titanium alloy memory wire.
