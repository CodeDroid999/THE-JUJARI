Product Features:

65W Charging Speed - Ultra-fast Charging
This Type-C charger supports power output 65W max and can fast charge up to 100% for Macbook Pro/Air 2022 in 2 hours.

GaN Tech - Less Heat, More Power
By swapping out silicon for gallium nitride (GaN), we're able to increase efficiency, improve heat dissipation, and reduce the size of our chargers.

From Phone to Laptop - Wide Compatibility
AniFast™ supports most charging protocol on the market and low current mode, can quickly charge almost all your devices.

Ultra-compact Size - Space-saving Design
Incredibly compact size for enhanced portability. All this power comes installed in a pocket-friendly compact body, to go wherever the journey takes you.

Multi-protection - Safety Reliable
Intelligent circuit design protects against short circuiting, over-heating, over-currents, and over-charging, when the battery is fully charged, it will automatically switch to protected mode.
