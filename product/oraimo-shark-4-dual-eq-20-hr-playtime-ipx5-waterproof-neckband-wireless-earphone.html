20-hr Playtime & Quick Charge--Play All Day

Dual Device Connection--Freely Switch to Another Device
Shark 4 can connect 2 devices at the same time. Never worry about missing the calls from another phone.

*Long press the Multi-functional button "O" and volume up button "M" at the same time for 2 seconds to connect to the second device.
