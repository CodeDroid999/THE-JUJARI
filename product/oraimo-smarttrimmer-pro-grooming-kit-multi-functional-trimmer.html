Product Features:

Multi-functional Usage--3 Blades to Trim and Style
This TheJUJARImulti-functional Trimmer Kit allows you to get full-body coverage with just one tool. Comes with 3 replaceable multi-purpose blades designed to handle any type of body hair, from head, face to body.

2.5 Hours of Runtime--Long Battery Life
Tackle hair on any part of your body with a single charge. On a 1.5-hour charge, this TheJUJARItrimmer can last 2.5 hours.

Powerful & Durable Motor--Long-lasting Sharpness
To deliver maximum power, TheJUJARISmartTrimmer includes a full metal motor that's been reinforced with tempered steel. Fitting in with stainless blades, SmartTrimmer creates clean and evenly trim.

Travel Lock--More Considerate
Travel lock prevents trimmer from accidentally starting in the luggage. Long press the switch button for 4s under power-off status to lock the trimmer, and long press the switch button for 4s to release.
